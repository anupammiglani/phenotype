The shell offered us the element of 'the arch'. From this we created the singular experience of 'walking through an endless colonnade'.The concrete envelope provides the stark stage for the 'event'. The brass arch wireframe interacts with the mirrors to create an illusion of multitude and continuity, extending the perceptive boundaries of the space.At any point within the store, you will feel like you are in a larger volume than the physical boundary of the space. Wehave combined the brass arch, mirror and concrete in different ways to create different experiences. For instance, outside the trial room we have used concentric mirrors to create another kind of 'depth perception'. The grid format of the layout allows for flexibility in the number of display racks. Provisional racks can be added as required on special occasions, reconfiguring the space, without ever losing its essence. The mirror alsohelps reflect the natural light, allowing an airiness through the space.
