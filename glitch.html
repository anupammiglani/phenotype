This digital marketing agency has a young team and very transitory patterns of working. They required an assortment of break out spaces, workstations, a design studio and brainstorm areas. The main theme of the space is a „park‟ which is a place for fun and games and creativity and a place where people come together and interact. This open plan office hosts 90 people without coming across as mundane or sterile.We have used rustic and natural elements to provide relief from a „box‟ environment.
